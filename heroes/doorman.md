# Doorman
Last Updated May 22nd

## Call Bell (1)

- Impact Damage/Explosion Damage is now 55/40 *(was 40/55)*

***Dev Note:** We decided to shift power from explosion damage to impact damage to promote early game skill shots to reward more skilled players.*

- Impact Damage/Explosion Damage Spirit scaling reduced to 0.5/1.1 *(was 0.7/1.4)*

***Dev Note:** This is to lower the scaling the ability does in the early game especially in regards to the vitality changes over the past few months*

- T1 now grants **+1 Charge**



- T2 now grants **+20 Impact Damage +30 Explosion Damage** *(was +30 Impact Damage +40 Explosion Damage)*

***Dev Note:** This change is primarily targeted to lower his early power spike in lane with a combination of this T2 and Mystic Burst. This T2 ensures you are able activate Mystic Burst on both Impact or Explosion but keeps the damage lower on average the "spirit nukers" unless you hit both Impact and explosion*

- T3 now grants **+2m radius, +26 Bell Lifetime, and Improved Scaling(+0.4/+0.4)** *(was +4m Radius, 26s Bell Lifetime, and Improved Spirit Scaling +0.4/+0.4)*

***Dev Note:** The only change was a radius nerf due to how radius scales with ability range. 14.4 meters made bells impossible to miss but also extremely spammable in choke points. this wont kill it but will lower its effectiveness*

## Hotel Guest (4)

- Failure to Check Out punishment is now **2 Second stun** *(was 125/275 spirit damage with 1.5x spirit scaling)*

***Dev Note:** This change is to both make room for the New AP Boon upgrades while also making FtCO punishment more based on the debuffs that occur rather than the pure damage taken*

- Can now Alt-Fire cast on allies to invite them to stay at the baroness as a VIP Guest. VIP Guest stay lasts 10 seconds and can be cancelled anytime by refusing service *By Pressing Your Ability Cancel Button*. During VIP Stays stamina recharges 50% faster, increases your **Current** Regen by 200%, and **ALL** debuffs are removed 50% faster and don't take their effects. Upon a full stay VIP’s will leave with a complimentary 10% of max health Barrier, that scales with spirit power 0.04, +2m Move Speed, +15 Spirit power. These complimentary buffs are bound to the barrier

***Dev Note:** This is to give doorman another displacement support option but instead of affecting enemy heroes benefiting ally heroes. Allowing for more team play and support in his special way that is both familiar and fair*

- T1 now grants **+150 damage and -30 Second Cooldown** *(was 1 stamina Drain and -25 Second Cooldown)*

***Dev Note:** With the removal of FtCO damage this gives his ult a little boost in both damage and usage frequency to remain competitive to other ult points*

- T2 now grants **Drains all stamina on Failure to Check Out. Doubles the effects of VIP Guest Stays** *(was +150 Damage. +1.5s Stun on Failure to Check Out)*

***Dev Note:** This change is to increase the punish capabilities of the ultimate when used to isolate enemy heroes while also making its support capabilities a lot stronger*

- T3 now grants **-60% Cooldown on Failure to Check Out. -40% Cooldown On VIP Full Stay. Prevents ability and item usage in the last 2.5 seconds of a guests stay** *(was Unstoppable while the Baroness is occupied. 13s Cooldown on Failure to Check Out)*

***Dev Note:** Making is % based rather than a hard number ensures that ULT CD scales properly as the game progresses. This also prevents characters like Mina, Pocket, Dynamo, and Ethereal Shift users to bypass the stun for as this ult is more so used as a punish positioning tool rather than an execute*
